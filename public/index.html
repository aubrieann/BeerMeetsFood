Crafted: Beer Meets Food

Crafted is a city-specific desktop and mobile-friendly application. Interest in craft beer has exploded in recent years and, as a result, specialty, small-scale breweries have popped up in many major cities. Crafted allows you to choose a local brew from your favorite local breweries and pair that choice with carefully selected recipes. The recipes are based on each beer's unique flavor profile.

Lots of apps offer food pairings for general beer types (such as porters and IPAs), but only Crafted offers pairing combinations unique to the beer you're drinking.

The app is currently specific to downtown Detroit's thriving local breweries, but has plans to extend to other major cities throughout the U.S.
